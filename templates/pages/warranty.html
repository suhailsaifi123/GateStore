GateStore Warranty

Peace of mind with every gate. Our products are backed by a comprehensive warranty for your security and satisfaction.

What's Covered?

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Material and workmanship issues
  • Structural integrity (frame, welds, joints)
  • Paint/finish peeling within warranty period

How to Claim

  1. Contact us via the Contact Page with your order details and issue description.
  2. Attach clear photos of the defect/problem.
  3. Our team will review and respond within 2 business days.
  4. If approved, we will arrange repair, replacement, or service as per policy.

Warranty FAQ

Our standard warranty is 2 years from the date of purchase. Please check your invoice for details.
